Joan of Arc!

Joan of Arc was a peasant girl at the age of 16 when she asked to have an army and to march into battle against the English. At this time, the French were being decimated by the English. The french had no other option but to allow a peasant to fight for them. Joan of Arc claimed to be a messenger of god and promised to bring victory to the French and defeat to the English. She fought for 3 years and allowed the French to claim back some of their land. Eventually, she was captured and was killed at the age of 19.

Joan of arc had lead troops into battle. She lead them to victory during the 100 years war against the English. She was most well known for the siege of Orleans. Joan showed leadership throughout the war and was well documented for it.

By joan of arc being a good solder, she was able to get Charles VII to become king of france. By being victorious during the battle of Oreanes, she got Charles VII crowned. With her victors on the battlefield she made France a better place during the 100 years war.
